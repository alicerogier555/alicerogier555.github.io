Building upon recent encounters and discussions, I am currently writing 📝 a new project "CONTICOG: Seguimiento longitudinal del deterioro cognitivo relacionado con la quimioterapia mediante datos digitales centrados en el paciente", to initiate a collaboration between ITiSB and the Faculty of Rehabilitation Science, and in particular with Falonn Contreras-Osorio and
Karol Ramírez-Paradal. The goal of this project is to develop a tool and implement it to measure cognitive impairments 🧠 during the chemotherapy treatment 💊. In parallel, I will further develop ontological tools to represent chemotherapy building upon my PhD work.
