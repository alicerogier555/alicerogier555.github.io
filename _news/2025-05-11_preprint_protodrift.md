I made available the preprint of my new article "ProtoDrift: Evaluating the impact of small-to-big adjustments to chemotherapy protocols". In it, we introduce *ProtoDrift*, a novel metric that quantifies time and dose adjustments in chemotherapies. This weighted distance enables an assessment of their impact on patient outcomes, offering more detailed analyses than the traditional Relative Dose Intensity (RDI). If you want more details, have a look at the [paper]({% link assets/pdf/publications/27_protodrift.pdf %})!
